Hey, I'm Gowtham, a software engineer in Bengaluru. What I actually enjoy is the unglamorous gap between a research idea and something that runs: teaching an agent to stop and ask before it deletes someone's work, getting a database to read fast enough that nobody notices it, shipping the feature the night before it's due.

Before that I built fintech systems at Wealthy. For fun I've written a storage engine in Go, shipped a few open-source tools, and once pulled off a 6,500-person college fest. I go deep when it counts, and I like finishing what I start.
